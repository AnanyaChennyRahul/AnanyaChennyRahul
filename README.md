## Featured Work

### Computer Vision and Deep Learning

**Steel Surface Defect Detection and Generalization**  
Built a controlled computer vision research project to study whether defect classifiers learn true defect semantics or rely on geometry and context shortcuts. The project involved synthetic non-flat steel surface generation, manual annotation for YOLO-based defect detection, CNN and transformer-based classification, cross-geometry evaluation, Grad-CAM, TSNE, representation analysis, and interpretability experiments.

**Traffic Sign Detection and Recognition**  
Developed a traffic sign recognition pipeline using CNNs and YOLO-based object detection. The project involved manually annotated samples, detection and classification experiments, evaluation, and an end-to-end detection plus recognition workflow.

**Medieval OCR / Historical Text Recognition**  
Worked on OCR for historical manuscript line images using CRNN-CTC baselines and transformer-based TrOCR models. Explored decoding strategies, metadata-aware analysis, script and language effects, and error patterns using WER and CER.

---

### RAG, NLP, and AI Applications

**JobMatch AI — AI-Powered Job Search and Resume Matching Platform**  
Built a full-stack RAG-based job matching platform that retrieves relevant jobs, ranks them, and generates explainable recommendations using resume/profile input, vector search, and LLM reasoning.

**Financial Statement Analyzer**  
As a part of my internship I worked on the core parser for extracting structured financial metrics from financial statement PDFs. The parser included document extraction logic, statement type detection, financial field matching, currency and scale detection, numeric cleaning, OCR-related parsing workflows, and structured JSON output. 

---

### Economics and Policy Research

**Does Learning in the Local Language Improve Local Outcomes?**  
Worked on an econometrics research project studying whether education in the local language is associated with improved local outcomes. The project involved framing an empirical research question, working with data, identifying relevant variables, and applying econometric methods to study the relationship between language of instruction and development outcomes.

**The Effect of the 2016 Bihar Alcohol Ban on Women's Autonomy**  
Conducted policy research on the impact of Bihar's 2016 alcohol prohibition on women's autonomy. Presented this work at IHEPA 2025, a fully funded conference. The project strengthened my interest in causal inference, public policy, gender outcomes, and evidence-based evaluation.
